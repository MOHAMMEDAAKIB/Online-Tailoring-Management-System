Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?

expand_more

Once your order is shipped, you will receive an email with a tracking number and a link to the carrier's website. You can also find the tracking information in the 'My Orders' section of your profile.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

expand_more

Unfortunately, once an order is placed, it cannot be modified. You may be able to cancel your order within 30 minutes of placing it. Please check the 'My Orders' page for cancellation options.

How do I update my measurements?

expand_more

You can update your measurements at any time by navigating to the 'Profile' section of your account. Please note that changes will only apply to future orders.

What is your return policy?

expand_more

We offer a 30-day return policy for any items that do not meet your satisfaction, provided they are in their original condition. Custom-tailored garments may have specific return conditions. Please visit our full 'Returns' page for details.
